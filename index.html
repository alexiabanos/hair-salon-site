Meet Minh

Minh classically trained as a stylist at the prestigious Vidal Sassoon Academy in Santa Monica, California.

In addition, Minh has received industry gold standard certifications, including becoming a Goldwell Master Colorist and a Natural Beaded Rows Extension Specialist. Always eager to evolve, Minh continuously enhances her skills by attending academy classes and collaborating with esteemed brands like KMS and Oribe.

In the salon, she enjoys working with blondes, creating dimensional colors using techniques such as balayage and color melting to create perfectly effortless lived-in hair.
