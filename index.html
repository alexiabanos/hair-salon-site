Meet Minh

Minh's journey into the world of hair styling began at the prestigious Vidal Sassoon Academy in Santa Monica, California, where she received her classical training in hair cutting. Her quest for excellence then led her to Chicago, where she completed the rigorous ABC’s of Cutting program, renowned for its emphasis on precision.

But Minh didn't stop there. Her passion for hair styling propelled her to become an expert in color treatments, earning her the title of certified Goldwell Master Colorist after comprehensive training with Goldwell. Always eager to evolve, Minh continuously enhances her skills by attending academy classes and collaborating with esteemed brands like KMS and Oribe.
